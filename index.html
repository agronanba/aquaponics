What is Aquaponics?

Aquaponics is an innovative farming technique that combines aquaculture (raising fish) and hydroponics (growing plants without soil) in a symbiotic environment. The waste produced by the fish supplies nutrients for the plants, while the plants help purify the water for the fish.

About the Project

This project aims to develop an AI-driven monitoring and control system for aquaponics agriculture. It focuses on optimizing water quality, monitoring plant health, and ensuring efficient fish feeding to maximize production and sustainability.
